一.功能句写作（命题作文）

(一)话题引起

1.人们慢慢认识到….

There is a growing worldwide/national awareness of + （主题词）n/doing

tip：an increasing global , international , concern over (往往倾向于负面话题或批判类话题)

  • 2008.一次性塑料袋使用的问题
  • There is a growing worldwide awareness of the problem of disposable(不可分解/降解) plastic bags among the public.
  • 2013.06 人们慢慢意识到学习基本技能的重要性.
  • There is a growing worldwide awareness of the importance of learning basic skills,

2.~~~引起了人们广泛的讨论

There has been a heated discussion about …sth/doing sth

tip: wide/deep/profound talk/debate/concern

  • 2011.06 在线购物 online shopping

  • There has been a heated discussion about (the impact of ) online shopping.

  • 2012.06 Excessive packaging 过度包装

  • There has been a heated discussion about (the harm of ) excessive packaging.

  • 2013.12 网络对学习的影响

  • There has been a heated discussion about the impact of the Internet on learning.

    tip: the impact of A on B

3.当前，有这样一种增长的趋势是：…

  • There is a growing tendency nowadays that + 句子…

tip: trend at present=now

  • 2011.6 网购

    • There is a growing tendency nowadays that more and more people are buying goods online.

    tip：an increasing number of –

    • people in mounting numbers are purchasing products on the internet.

4.近来，…的问题引发了…关注.

  • 201.06 过度包装

  • Recently,the problem of “excessive packaging （主题词）” has aroused wide/deep/great/profound（深刻的） concern among the public.

    tip: arouse（引发）~~provoke（激发/唤起）

    • Recently—In recent years / decade,

    • Problem—Issue/ situation / phenomenon

  • 2013.12 低头族

    • Recently , the problem of “phubbing “ has aroused wide/deep/great concern among the public.

      tip: phubbing == phone + snub(冷漠) rely on mobilephone

5.无人不知，无人不晓

  • Nobody could have failed to notice the fact that … has become a grave（-负） problem / popular （+正）phenomenon in China.
    • 2017.12 医患关系
    • Nobody could failed to notice the fact that the relationship between doctors and patients has become a grave problem in China.
